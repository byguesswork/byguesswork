Wormholing

Feb 2025



Back in the eighties and nineties I rememeber some computer games (on Amiga and PC, perhaps also back on Commodore 64, I don't remember exactly) involved navigating a first-person view through the twists and turns of a wormhole somewhere in space.

It's been a long time I had this wish to try and recreate such sort of gameplay feature (that is, the rudimentary mechanics of it).

In trying to do so it turned out that the first idea on how to approach the thing was not the correct one, not the second one ... so there are different takes on it on the links on this page.
