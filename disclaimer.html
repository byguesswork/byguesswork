Whole site

The site does not collect, use, store or transfer any personal data about the visitor nor does it collect, use, store or transfer any data about the device visiting the site.



Cookies

No cookies for you. If you play Tetris, a high scores list is kept (more below), but that is not a cookie.



Tetris

The game keeps a high score list. The highscores are kept in your local browser in which you visit the site, not on any server. So the highscores you see are your own highscores and they do not include any highscores of any other person who might have visited this site, played a game and reached a high score.



Sorta Paint

The application offers the possibility to download the doodle you created. The image is downloaded to your Downloads folder on your local machine.
