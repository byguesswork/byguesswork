Monthly-daily Bill Evans - Peace Piece

November 2025

Ah, November rain. No, not the Guns N'Roses song. It was never my favorite, despite the ambitious and pompous video featuring epic scenes from an orchestra hall, a wedding, a funeral and Slash doing Slash in front of a tiny wind-swept church in the middle of the desert. Of the longer takes on the double album I would always prefer Coma on odd days and Estranged on even days, and sometimes Civil War would make it spot nr. 3 before the song featuring precipitations in its title.

November rain, the dreary, dark times in late autumn, with slimy weather and raindrops hesitantly sliding down the window, racing each other which one can linger on the glass for longer. A downbeat time, unless spent of course with company you love and that brings you happiness, perhaps by a cozy Café table, lazily sipping hot tea.

A good antidote for the heavy times is also this piece. Like an instant switch it unfailingly stops the racing mind, perhaps the worries too. Timeless magic.



Ivo Makuc, 2025
