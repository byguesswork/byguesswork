Two programs I wrote since I started to learn JavaScript and HTML as a hobby.

Tetris:
Try out playing Tetris in different directions (or in random direction mode) or wiping out several lines at a time

Sorta paint:
A rudimentary program for drawing (it was my test polygon when learning how to manipulate stuff on the screen).
Currently only in Slovenian (no other reason than that is my mother language)

Why "By guesswork"? Because when working on the programs, the process felt like a mix of guesswork,
trial and error style, regular course correction and learning as you go. Enjoyed the whole of it!
