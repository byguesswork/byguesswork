More about the programs:

Leftris:
Online Tetris game that can be played with the pieces falling upwards or to the left (or optionally with surprise changes of gameplay direction).
Check out the effects after you wipe out several lines at a time or try playing on a grid of a different size.
Not mobile device friendly (Needs a keyboard).


Sorta paint:
A rudimentary program for drawing, a test polygon when I was learning how to work with stuff on the screen.
Some parts of the interface might be in Slovenian (no other reason than that is my mother language and was the working language when doing the UI).


Classifier neural network:
An attempt to a classifier neural network that classifies Slovene names by gender. Not a serious product and definitely no claims about the results, just a program with which I was learning to create and train a neural network. UI in Slovene only and has been trained on Slovene names only.




The why behind name "byGuesswork"
When learning to code, the journey was sometimes smooth sailing, sometimes going nowhere for sessions, sometimes bouncing me from one head-scratching puzzle into the next. Probably the usual when you throw yourself into something completely new. In the midst of it, I often got aware of a feeling, or a thought, that the way things moved forward was by guesswork (not really of the lost, but of the curious kind).
A most enjoyable learning adventure. (It still is.)
