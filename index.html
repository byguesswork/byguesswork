LEFTRIS, UPTRIS, RIGHTRIS ... TETRIS!

Try out playing Tetris in various directions (or in random mode, with surprise changes of direction in which the game is played).
Check out the effects after you wipe out several lines at a time or try playing on a grid of a different size.
Not mobile device friendly (Needs a keyboard).

Sorta paint

A rudimentary program for drawing (it was my test polygon when learning how to manipulate stuff on the screen).
Some parts of the interface might be in Slovenian (no other reason than that is my mother language and so far I haven't managed to localize all parts yet).
Not mobile device friendly (Needs a keyboard).

Slovene names classifier neural network

An attempt to a classifier neural network that classifies Slovene names by gender. Not a serious product and definitely no claims about the results, just a program with which I was learning to create and train a neural network. UI in Slovene only and has been trained on Slovene names only. Not mobile device friendly (Prefers a larger screen).

Let's play! - Android App

An Android App with descriptions of 150+ Indoor and Outdoor Games.
Do you ever find yourself in a situation where you and your friends are up for a parlor game but you just can't think of one in that moment? Or perhaps you are a parent and have a company of kids over who have energy to spare and you would like to offer them an idea for an outdoor game?
Check out the free "Let's Play" for some ideas.
