Two programs I wrote since I started to learn JavaScript and HTML.

Tetris:
Try out playing Tetris in different directions (or in random direction mode) or wiping out several lines at a time.
Does not like mobile and is not really a friend of a screen that is too small

Sorta paint:
A rudimentary program for drawing. (Actually a test polygon to learn about manipulating stuff on the screen.)
Currently only in Slovenian (no other reason than that is my mother language)

Why "By guesswork"? Because the learning was nothing but a mix of guesswork, trial and error style, with
a side of determination (plus obviously the irreplacable main dish in the form of a great online JS course).
Enjoyed the whole of it!
