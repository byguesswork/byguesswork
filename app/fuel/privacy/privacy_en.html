The only data that the App stores on User's Device is data needed for the functioning of the App itself. The only data that might be transferred outside the User's Device is data that might be generated and subsequently transferred from the Device by the Google Play ecosystem/infrastructure, ie. data related to eventual App crash reports, errors in App functioning and similar. The App itself does not generate, collect, use or transmit such data.
