Who knows what excactly play is for, but seemingly it is quite a consequential kink in the evolution of animals. It's the stuff through which a fluffy kitten turns into an agile predator, feared by all ... well, mice, and through which a toddler starts to exert his or her curiosity, to explore reality and to hone his or her skills which will later allow them to be a proficient engineer or teacher or nurse or firefighter.

There's no shortage of reasons why engaging in play is beneficial. Play is playful. Play is exploration, experimentation, testing. Play is creativity. Play teaches you to be attentive, to connect with own senses, feelings and intentions. Play is playful gathering of experiences and skills that will be important later in life.

Play is socializing. With play we learn how to understand others, deal with own emotions and those of others, how to behave in a social context, how to resolve disputes. Play is learning how to cooperate with others. Play is learning how to work together towards a common goal. Play is learning how to get around a shared vision and work out a strategy for bringing it to life.

Play is experiencing the joy and pleasure of a win. Play is learning how to cope with a loss. Play is learning how rules and instructions work and how to work with them. Play is discovering how it feels to come up with own rules to adapt existing games or come up with new ones. Play is the joy of unabridged imagination let loose to savor the world one finds themselves in. Play is the ultimate playground for imagination and brings happiness and joy to body, mind and soul.

So … Let's play!


An App containing descriptions and instructions for 150+ Indoor and Outdoor Games

Do you ever find yourself in a situation where you and your friends are up for a parlor game but you just can't think of a suitable one?
Or perhaps you are a parent and have a company of kids over who have energy to spare and would like to offer them an idea for an outdoor game?

The "Let's Play (Indoor&Outdoor Games)" app can help you out. Just select your venue (indoors or outdoors) and the number of players and you'll get a list of suitable games, with a short description and instructions for each.

App characteristics:

  • -   Contains descriptions of 150-200 no-tech outdoor and parlor games (number of games depending on phone language setting)
  • -   App interface and games descriptions currently available in English and Slovene (new languages to be added)
  • -   Contains well known games played around the world as well as traditional games from Slovenia played by folks in times past.
